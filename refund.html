Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident in our work. If you're not satisfied with your delivered design, we offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked.

What's Covered

Our Money-Back Guarantee covers:

  • Full refund of service fees if you're unsatisfied with the final design
  • All design deliverables from your package
  • Up to 2 rounds of free revisions to meet your expectations

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us at info@fastgraphicdesign.co.uk
  2. Provide your order number and reason for requesting a refund
  3. We will process your refund within 5-7 business days

Revision Process Before Refund

Before requesting a refund, we encourage you to work with us on revisions. Your package includes:

  • 2 rounds of free revisions
  • Direct communication with the design team
  • Multiple design concepts to choose from

Refund Conditions

Refunds are available if:

  • The request is made within 30 days of purchase
  • You have provided clear feedback about your concerns
  • We've had the opportunity to address your issues through revisions

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refund:

  • Custom work beyond the scope of the original project brief
  • Rush or expedited delivery fees
  • Services provided more than 30 days ago

Questions about our refund policy? Contact us at info@fastgraphicdesign.co.uk or call during business hours.
